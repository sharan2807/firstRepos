Welcome to MAPS Workout plan



Monday: Upper Body Focus

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching
Pull-ups: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
Dips: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
Dumbbell rows (or bodyweight rows): 3 sets of 8-12 reps
Shoulder press (or pike push-ups): 3 sets of 8-12 reps
Bicep curls (or chin-ups): 3 sets of 8-12 reps
Tricep dips: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
Cool-down: 5-10 minutes of static stretching


Wednesday: Lower Body Focus

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching
Bodyweight squats: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
Lunges (or Bulgarian split squats): 3 sets of 8-12 reps per leg
Calf raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Glute bridges (or hip thrusts): 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
Cool-down: 5-10 minutes of static stretching
